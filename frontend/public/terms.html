1. Description of Service

SlabClaw is a tool for vintage Pokemon card collectors (WOTC era, 1996–2003). The App provides:

  • AI-powered card grade predictions using computer vision
  • Cross-platform price comparison across tokenized and physical marketplaces
  • Portfolio tracking and valuation
  • Arbitrage detection and deal alerts
  • Expected value calculations for grading decisions
